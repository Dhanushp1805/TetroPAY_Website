All-in-One HR & Payroll Features for Modern Businesses

Manage attendance, payroll, employee data, and performance — everything in one powerful HRMS platform.

Recruitment Management

Streamline hiring from job posting to candidate selection

TetroPAY makes recruitment effortless with a centralized hiring dashboard. HR teams can create job postings, track applications, shortlist candidates, schedule interviews, and record feedback — all within one system. The built-in candidate tracker ensures no applicant is missed, and hiring becomes faster, transparent, and well-organized.

Onboarding

Deliver a smooth, paperless joining experience

Once a candidate is selected, TetroPAY simplifies onboarding with digital forms and automated workflows. Employees can upload documents, fill personal details, and receive induction materials online. Departments get notified instantly, enabling quick provisioning of email IDs, devices, and access rights. This reduces manual effort and helps new hires start their journey with clarity and confidence.

Employee Information Management

A complete digital profile for every employee

TetroPAY stores all employee details in one place — personal information, job role, department, documents, salary structure, and historical records. The system provides quick search and filtering options, making data retrieval instant. No more spreadsheets or manual files; everything is securely organized and accessible anytime.

Attendance & Shift Management

Accurate attendance with automation and flexibility

TetroPAY supports multiple attendance modes including:

  • Web login
  • Mobile GPS check-in/out
  • QR code scanning
  • Biometric integration

HR can create custom shifts, weekly offs, and rosters. Late marks, permissions, overtime, and shift rotations are calculated automatically. Real-time reports help managers maintain workforce discipline and reduce discrepancies.

Leave Management

Automated leave policies with smooth approval workflows

Create unlimited leave types, set accrual rules, holiday lists, and LOP rules exactly as per your company policy. Employees apply from ESS, managers approve with one click, and the system updates balances instantly. Audit trails, leave history, and carry forward rules are fully automated to avoid confusion or manual errors.

Payroll Management

End-to-end salary automation with complete compliance

TetroPAY handles every part of salary calculation — basic pay, allowances, overtime, deductions, PF, ESI, PT, TDS, reimbursements, advances, and incentives. With one-click payroll, payslips are generated instantly and can be downloaded by employees. The system ensures statutory compliance, reduces manual errors, and saves hours of HR time every month.

Employee Self-Service (ESS)

Give employees the power to manage their own HR data

The ESS portal allows employees to:

  • Download payslips
  • View attendance
  • Apply for leave
  • Update personal details
  • Upload documents
  • Track approvals

This brings transparency, reduces HR workload, and improves employee satisfaction.

Performance & AI Insights

Smarter appraisals with real-time performance analytics

TetroPAY replaces manual review cycles with intelligent dashboards. Track employee performance, skills, achievements, and productivity trends. AI-driven insights help managers identify high performers, training requirements, and potential risks — enabling strategic HR decisions.

Geo Map Tracking

Real-time monitoring for field employees

Field staff can check-in and check-out with GPS. Managers can view live locations, travel paths, and client visit details on the map. This improves visibility, prevents misuse of time, and helps plan routes better.

Document Management

A secure digital storage system for all HR files

Upload, organize, and store employee documents with categories and tags. Set reminders for document expiry (ID cards, contracts, licenses). Access permissions ensure only authorized users can view or download sensitive files.

Reports & Analytics

Access 50+ HR reports with real-time insights

Generate detailed reports on:

  • Payroll
  • Attendance
  • Leave
  • Overtime
  • Headcount
  • Compliance
  • Employee joining & exit

Reports can be downloaded in Excel, PDF, or CSV formats for audits and internal reviews.

Notifications & Alerts

Never miss an update with smart alerts

TetroPAY sends instant notifications through:

  • WhatsApp
  • Email
  • SMS

For approvals, announcements, attendance alerts, payroll updates, and important reminders — ensuring everyone stays informed.

Exit & Full & Final Settlement

Complete exit process handled smoothly

Manage resignations with automated acceptance, notice period calculations, recovery handling, asset clearance, and relieving letters. The Full & Final Settlement automatically calculates pending salary, leaves encashment, deductions, and generates the settlement statement. Exit becomes clean, compliant, and fast.

TetroPAY is a smart HR & Payroll platform built to automate and simplify HR operations.
